Cleaning the dates¶

After taking a look at the dates, it looks like we have mixed dates from when the complaint was first filed. To keep everything consistent, we decided to remove CMPLNT_TO_DT and CMPLNT_FR_DT observations before 2019. Moreover, since we are interested in when the complaint happened, we will be looking into the CMPLNT_FR_DT and have created attributes separating the year, month, day, and weekday.

In [ ]:
# Making the dates datetime objects.
df['CMPLNT_FR_DT'] = pd.to_datetime(df['CMPLNT_FR_DT'], errors='coerce') 
df['CMPLNT_TO_DT'] = pd.to_datetime(df['CMPLNT_TO_DT'])
df['RPT_DT'] = pd.to_datetime(df['RPT_DT'])

df = df[~(df[‘CMPLNT_FR_DT’] < ‘2019-01-01’) & ~(df[‘CMPLNT_TO_DT’] < ‘2019-01-01’)] # Removing dates before 2019 in both CMPLNT_FR_DT and CMPLNT_TO_DT
